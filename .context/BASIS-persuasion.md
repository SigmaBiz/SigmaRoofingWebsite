## The engine (the distilled basis)

The four primitives are a **2×2**, cut by two axes:

|                        | **LEVEL** (static — where it IS)            | **RATE** (moving — where it's GOING)        |
|------------------------|---------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------|
| **ENERGY** (the stake) | **STATUS** — reserve held; being seen/judged| **THREAT** — capital bleeding; boundary breached |
| **INFORMATION** (gap)  | **CONTRADICTION** — "your snapshot is false"| **IRONY** — "your movie is false"           |

**Three laws** govern how they're used (all consequences of the origin — a matched filter,
evolved, real):

1. **Static < dynamic.** A rate-signal out-pulls a level-signal. THREAT beats STATUS; IRONY
   beats CONTRADICTION — when the material is honestly true to it.
2. **Landing is an inner product onto a *real, installed* detector.** You never *create* fear
   or status — they're pre-wired from selection. You present a signal that projects onto them.
3. **Attention = the energy spent driving a prediction-error back to zero.** No gap aimed at
   *their own* ledger → nothing to spend → bounce.

**Honesty floor (a theorem, not a rule).** Because the detector is real and tuned, a
*fabricated* signal has no real detector to land on → it is detected and routed around. So
every lever must be **true leverage the homeowner actually carries.** This is also why
**State B (spectacle) is forbidden**: spectacle-without-substance resolves to the wrong sign
("trying too hard → scam") and fails the not-a-scammer detector. Care-density is mandatory,
not decorative — it's the real signal everything else projects onto.

## The homeowner instantiation (real signals only)

Aim the four at the OKC post-hail homeowner, on a phone, asking *is this real / can I trust
them / how fast can I book*:

- **CONTRADICTION — the visual hook. "Looks fine ≠ is fine."** Hail damage is frequently
  invisible from the ground. Cause present (the storm crossed your address), effect absent
  (you see nothing) — and that absence is the trap, not the all-clear. Physically true,
  legally clean, and it's a *picture* (ground-view vs. close-up). → lands in **imagery + the reveal.**
- **IRONY — the headline grab (RATE; the sharpest).** Everywhere else, the prudent move is
  *don't fix what isn't visibly broken* — a model that's right, which is why it's trusted.
  On a hail-struck roof its sign inverts: waiting for visible proof is the exact mechanism
  that costs them the roof (window closes, attribution decays, they pay). Mechanism of
  success = mechanism of failure. → lands in the **headline.** Draft seed: *"Everywhere else,
  waiting until it breaks saves you money. On a hail-struck roof, waiting is what costs you
  the whole thing."*
- **THREAT — the energy under everything (RATE, −, ~40% of voltage, fires before cognition).**
  The roof is the boundary over family + the biggest asset; post-storm it may be breached
  invisibly with a clock running. → lands in **copy + personalization aimed at THEIR address.**
  The existing address-autocomplete is the **aiming instrument** (makes it land on "your"
  situation), not a form field. ⚠ The claim-window/clock mechanics are regulated and
  OK-specific — see the flag below.
- **STATUS — the sustain (LEVEL, +, the gaze).** Their home is *seen* — by the street, the
  future buyer (disclosed at sale), the insurer. Downside: being seen as the one who got
  fooled / missed it. Upside: the neighbor who already acted. → lands in **social proof**
  (reviews, neighbor photos, "your neighbors on [street] already had theirs documented").

**Attention → influence sequence:** open on the grab (IRONY headline + CONTRADICTION reveal)
→ settle the static gap (CONTRADICTION) → move it (IRONY) → load the energy (THREAT, on their
address) → sustain (STATUS) → **small reversible ask** (free inspection). The re-sign ("your
roof is an asset") is the SALE and is **withheld** — that's the porch, downstream.

## The brand nomination (downstream of the basis, not taste)

The skin has two jobs that pull against each other, and the basis names both:
1. **Clear the scam-detector** so any true signal can land at all (law 2: a distrusted source
   routes every signal around itself). → wants **warmth, care, cohesion.**
2. **Carry THREAT + STATUS gravity** — the storm, the boundary, the stakes. → wants **weight,
   authority, seriousness.**

Pure **warm-trust (rose/clay)** nails #1, under-delivers #2 (too soft for a named-peril threat).
**Storm-authority (deep navy/slate base + warm gold accent)** does both: navy carries the
gravity, warm gold clears the scam-detector and does the cradle. **Evolved-green is demoted
hardest** — it *is* the competitor template → near-zero prediction-error → invisible (law 3).

→ **The basis nominates storm-authority.** Verify by *render* (screenshots on the real home
page), judged against a warm-trust control — not by adjectives.

## Regulated-domain flag (honesty floor + compliance are the same constraint)

The institutional **THREAT** lever (claim windows, disclosure law, what a contractor may
legally say about a claim, deductible handling) is **OK-specific, regulated, and currently
unverified.** In many states a contractor acting as a public adjuster is illegal. So on the
website: **lead with CONTRADICTION + IRONY** (physically true, legally safe); carry the
institutional THREAT **carefully** and do not state claim mechanics as fact until grounded
against Oklahoma rules + qualified counsel. A fabricated/overstated claim is both detected by
the homeowner AND a compliance risk.

## Scope: what the basis governs

- **The SKIN** (this plan): the basis binds it *narrowly but critically* — own the OPEN grab
  (first-frame spike + de-threat), supply DIRECTION (rate, not a static gallery), obey the
  honesty floor (shown-not-declared), withhold the re-sign. The visual surface can't *run* the
  semantic tools (irony/contradiction live in copy + imagery), but it decides whether the
  visitor stays long enough for them to land.
- **The CONTENT** (a later, separate plan): the richer toolset — the IRONY/CONTRADICTION copy,
  the lien teaching, hail-calibration imagery — is where the heavy payoff sits. Legally gated
  (see flag). This is THE-DOOR §F territory, parked for now.
